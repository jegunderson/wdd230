Target Audience

Personas

Persona 1: Jeremy Gerkich

Jeremy Gerkich is in his late 20's he is a potential business owner. He is looking for the best city to start his business that will be the most profitable for himself as well as finding the right clientele. He will be looking for answers to all their questions about the economy of the city and the already existing businesses.

Person 2: Doug Dimmadome

Doug Dimmadome is a man in his mid fourties who already has his own business. He is interested in joining the chamber. He is the head of an organization concerned with the socioeconomic climate of their community. This person wants to meet with others because they know they have are more effective as part of a group than than trying to act alone.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Someone is looking to move their business to the Teton Valley and wants to feel confident in the existing businesses.

Scenario 2

A business owner is looking network with other local businesses.

Scenario 3

A new business is looking to get their name known and start some promotions.

Scenario 4

A member of the community is looking to see when upcoming social and business events are happening.

Scenario 5

A business owner who recently moved into town is wanting to join the local chamber.
